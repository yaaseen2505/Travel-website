Greece

Greece is a popular travel destination with many attractions such as ancient ruins, sunny beaches, mouth-watering cuisines, historical sites, stunning landscapes, and places of cultural importance. Some popular tourist destinations include Athens, Santorini, Mykonos, Corfu, Zakynthos, and Thessaloniki.

If you are planning a trip to Greece and want to explore Athens city in particular then you can visit Acropolis Museum which houses ancient artifacts from Acropolis hill.
